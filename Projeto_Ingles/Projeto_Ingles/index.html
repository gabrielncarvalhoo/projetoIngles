Support

The Unifacisa Support is a communication channel of a mediating and democratic nature, which welcomes the manifestations of students, teachers, employees, and the community in general. If you have any complaints, reports, suggestions, requests, or compliments, please contact us via our email: support@unifacisa.edu.br or, if you prefer, fill out our form by clicking on the link below.
